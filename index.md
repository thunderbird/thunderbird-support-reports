# Thunderbird Support Reports

Monthly support operations reports for Thunderbird Android, K-9 Mail, Desktop SUMO, and TB Pro.

## Current month (live)

In-progress data for the current month, refreshed hourly by automation:

- [Daily support report](https://thunderbird.github.io/thunderbird-support-reports/lisa/daily/latest.html) — live Thundermail / TB Pro support snapshot
- [Launch overview](https://thunderbird.github.io/thunderbird-support-reports/lisa/daily/launch_overview.html) — invite waves, volumes, and milestones

> The monthly reports below are finalized in the *following* month, once each month's data sources have fully closed. That's why a report's "generated" date falls in the month after the report period (e.g. the May report is generated in early June).
