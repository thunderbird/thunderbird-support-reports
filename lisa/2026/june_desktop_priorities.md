Three themes drawn from 750 June SUMO questions. Questions can appear under more than one theme. Sub-themes inside each theme are derived from title patterns seen in the data, so counts within a theme are illustrative (some questions hit multiple sub-themes; some land in "Other"). The headline theme totals are the load-bearing number.
