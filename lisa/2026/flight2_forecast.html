What to Watch

Week 2 watch: ~14 tickets/day (~5/person) as new subscribers hit setup friction — email config, alias setup, password issues. Distributed across 3 agents this is workable, but issue clustering (e.g. a new Thundermail bug) can concentrate load. Flag if 3+ tickets in 48h share the same root cause — escalate to product rather than letting the queue absorb it.
